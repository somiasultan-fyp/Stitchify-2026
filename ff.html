Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive my custom outfit?

Typically, it takes 2-3 weeks from order placement to delivery. This includes design consultation, tailoring, and quality checks. Rush orders may be available for an additional fee.

What if the outfit doesn't fit properly?

We offer free alterations within 30 days of delivery. Our tailors will make the necessary adjustments to ensure a perfect fit at no extra cost to you.

Can I provide my own fabric?

Yes, you can provide your own fabric. We recommend consulting with our tailors first to ensure the fabric is suitable for your desired design.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship within the country only. We're working on expanding our services internationally in the near future.
